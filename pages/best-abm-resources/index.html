Best Resources for Account-Based Marketing in 2026

Account-based marketing has matured from a buzzword into a standard operating model for B2B companies selling to enterprise accounts. The ecosystem of platforms, communities, and educational content has grown accordingly. But quality varies wildly.

This list focuses on the ABM resources that practitioners recommend. Platforms that actually work, communities with real practitioners (not just vendors selling), and content that teaches execution, not theory.

Blogs & Websites

1. Demandbase Blog & Resources

State of ABM reports, webinars, and research from the leading ABM platform.

2. 6sense Blog

ABM platform blog covering intent data, predictive analytics, and account-based strategies.

3. MarTech ABM Section

ABM news, trends, and how-to guides.

4. ABM Pulse OUR PICK

Account-based marketing intelligence and tool reviews.

Podcasts

1. Account-Based Marketing Podcast (Momentum ITSMA)

Monthly conversations with sales and marketing leaders sharing practical ABM tips.

Courses & Training

1. Demandbase Academy

Free ABM certifications and courses (GTM strategy, measurement). Self-paced, 4-6 hours each.

2. Momentum ITSMA ABM Certification

Industry-leading ABM certification. 3,000+ professionals trained. Global standard for ABM training.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.
